Stuffed Mushroom Recipe

Description

This sausage-stuffed mushroom recipe is not only gluten free, but delicious! Replacing panko crumbs with pork rinds, everyone can enjoy this dish.

This recipe can be modified to be vegetarian as well, just replace the sausage with mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 16 extra-large white mushrooms
  • 5 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 1/2 tablspoons Marsala wine
  • 3/4 pound seet italian sausage
  • 6 scallions, white and green parts minced
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 2/3 cup pork rinds
  • 5 ounces of marscapone cheese
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
    2. Remove the stems from mushrooms and chop. Set aside. Toss mushroom caps with Marsala wine and 3 tablespoons of olive oil
    3. Heat remaining olive oil. Cook sausage on medium-high heat for ten minutes. Add mushroom stems and cook for another three. Stir in scallions and garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add pork rinds. Add marscapone. Off the heat, stir in parmesan, salt, and pepper
    4. Fill each mushroom with sausage mixture. Arrange mushrooms on baking dish. Bake for 50 minuts, until stuffing is browned and crusty.
