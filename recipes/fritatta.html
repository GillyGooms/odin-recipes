Fritatta Recipe

Description

A fritatta is a classic breakfast dish that can be enjoyed by the whole family! Just a few easy steps and you have yourself a mouth-watering meal that is even better the next day!

This recipe calls for a cast iron skillet. If you do not have one, opt for a deep backing dish instead.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
    2. Place bacon in the skillet and cook to preerence. Drain bacon fat and set bacon aside.
    3. Whisk eggs, milk, melted butter, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Pour into skillet and sprinkle with bacon and cheese.
    4. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes. It is ready when a knife inserted near the center comes out clean
