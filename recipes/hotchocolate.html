Hot Chocolate Recipe

Description

This hot chocolate recipe is the perfect way to warm up with something sweet! A one-serving size recipe, making sure you don't overindulge the sweet tooth.

This recipe comes straight from Hershey Park and is sure to impress

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • salt
  • 2-3 teaspoons of Hershey's cocoa
  • 1 cup whole milk

    • Steps

    1. Mix sugar, cocoa, and salt in a large mug
    2. Heat milk in microwave for 1-1/2 minutes
    3. Ggradually add hot milk to cocoa in mug and stir until blended
    4. Stir in vanilla
